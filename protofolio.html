Features

Everything a modern
marketplace needs

🎬
XPLOR — Video Feed
TikTok-style vertical video feed where creators showcase products. Swipe, watch, and buy — all in one flow.
🪙
ApyCoins Loyalty
Earn 10 coins per order, 100 per referral. Redeem for discounts. Real loyalty that keeps users coming back.
🏪
Seller Dashboard
Full seller onboarding with KYC verification, product listing, earnings tracking, and withdrawal system.
⚡
Real-time Everything
Firebase Firestore listeners — product updates, order status, video approvals all sync instantly across devices.
🔐
Google Auth
One-tap Google sign-in with role-based access — User, Seller, Manager. Secure and frictionless.
🤖
AI-Powered Admin
ApyBot powered by Groq LLaMA3 — intelligent admin assistant with live marketplace context injection.
📦
Full Order Flow
Cart → Checkout → COD → Tracking → Return/Cancel — complete order lifecycle with WhatsApp confirmation.
📸
Cloudinary Media
Multi-image product galleries with canvas compression. Video upload with real-time progress tracking.
🎨
Dynamic Theming
4 admin-controlled themes (Dark/Light/Blue/Purple) with live branding updates — no redeploy needed.
